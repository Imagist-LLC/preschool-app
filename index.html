Preschool.app

A simple, carefully crafted learning app for ages 1–5.

Off to a beautiful start.

Good design matters—even for the youngest learners. Preschool.app features a clean interface, large buttons, and intuitive navigation. No distractions. No ads. No gimmicks. Just pure learning.

Colors that pop. And slide.

Explore the rainbow with vibrant visuals and playful animations. Every little tap and swipe invites discovery, with interactions designed to capture young imaginations while remaining simple and frustration-free.

Objects. Permanence.

From circles and squares to stars and hearts, children can explore objects and shapes, see real-world examples, and (coming soon) match them in an interactive game. Preschool.app makes learning both fun and memorable.

This app speaks to you. Not at you.

Tap a letter, hear its sound, and see it in use. No annoying sound effects—just clear, confident pronunciation that makes learning to read easy.

Designed to adapt.

From larger text and buttons to dark mode for reducing eye strain (and saving battery), Preschool.app adjusts to every hand, big or small.

We’re growing.

We’re just getting started. Future updates will include Numbers, Objects, Opposites, and Emotions—each designed with the same thoughtful approach. Stay tuned as Preschool.app grows with your child.
