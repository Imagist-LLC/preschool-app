Preschool.app

A thoughtfully crafted app for young learners.

Easy on the Eyes. And Ears.

Good design matters—especially for those just getting their hands dirty. Unlike so many apps made for kids, Preschool.app features a clean interface and gentle audio design. Little touches like dark mode help save battery and make screen time healthier.

Colors That Pop. And Bounce.

Explore the rainbow with vibrant visuals and joyful animations. Every little tap and swipe invites playful discovery while remaining simple and frustration-free.

Objects. Permanence.

From circles and squares to stars and hearts, children can explore objects and shapes, see real-world examples, and (coming soon) match them in an interactive game.

Speaks to You. Not at You.

Letters share their sounds, colors tell their names, and objects introduce themselves when tapped. No forced excitement or silly voices—just clear pronunciation that makes everything click.

Universal. By Design.

From Apple Watch to Apple TV, and everything in between, Preschool.app makes early learning accessible to all. Use a keyboard, touch screen, trackpad, or remote—it adapts to every young learner's needs.

A Parent's Code.

Preschool.app was written for our daughter, not a focus group. We don't want your data, so we don't ask for it. No ads. No tracking. No engagement loops. Just pure learning.

Growing Smarter.

We're off to a beautiful start, but this is just the beginning. Upcoming features will include Numbers, Objects, Opposites, and Emotions—each designed with the same thoughtful approach.
