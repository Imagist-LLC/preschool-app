Privacy Policy

Preschool.app

We respect your privacy and keep your data secure.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Preschool.app! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We designed Preschool.app to be safe, minimal, and private. We do not sell, share, or mine your data.

2. What Information We Collect

Currently, Preschool.app does not collect, store, or share personal information. However, in future updates, we may collect the following limited data to improve your experience:

  • First Name (Optional): To personalize the app experience. This may be collected through Sign in with Apple, which provides enhanced privacy protections.
  • Email Address (Optional): If you choose to share it through Sign in with Apple. You will always have the option to hide your email address.
  • Progress & Level Data: To save learning progress and adapt content.

We do not collect phone numbers, location data, or any sensitive information.

3. How We Use Your Information

If we store any information, it will be used only for:

  • ✓ Saving progress so users can resume learning where they left off.
  • ✓ Personalizing content to make the app more engaging.
  • ✓ Syncing your data across your devices (if you enable iCloud sync).

Your data will never be sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

All stored data will be private and used only within the app.

Data may be stored in one or more of the following ways:

  • Locally on your device
  • Securely in iCloud (if sync features are enabled)

If you choose to use iCloud for syncing, your data will be handled according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

No third-party advertisers or data trackers are used.

5. Third-Party Services

Preschool.app does not use third-party analytics, ads, or tracking services. If this changes, we will update this policy and notify you before implementing such services.

The only third-party service we may use is Apple's Sign in with Apple and iCloud, both of which are subject to Apple's strong privacy protections.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data:

  • You can delete any stored data at any time within the app settings.
  • If we introduce user accounts, you will have the right to export or delete your data.
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time.
  • We will notify users before collecting any new types of data.

7. Children's Privacy

Preschool.app is designed for young children with parent supervision. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • Any future features that might collect information will require parental consent.
  • Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.

Significant changes will be communicated via app notification or email (if provided).

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

hello@preschool.app
